My name is Lizeth Espana, second-year alumni at the University of California, Riverside. I am majoring in Pre-Business in hopes of working towards a concentration in accounting. My hometown is Downtown, Los Angeles where I currently live with both my parents and younger brother. As a first-generation Latina, I am the first one in my family to graduate high school and continue to post-secondary education. Throughout my high school years and first year of college, I have had an interest in pursuing a degree in Business specifically going into the field of accounting. Having an interest in math, working with numbers, and analyzing has made me decide that I want to pursue accounting where I would be able to put into practice these tasks. An accountant not only helps maintain important financial records but also makes sure that it follows regulations and laws. I believe that accounting is the major for me since I like clerical work and would also like to work for a company in which I would work with their finances and help people.
